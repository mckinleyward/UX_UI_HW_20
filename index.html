MyTrip

The simplest way to plan, book, and organize your next trip.

Duration

7 Weeks

Type

Independent student project completed while in OSU UX/UI Bootcamp.

Responsibilities

User research, personas, storyboarding, competitive analysis, sketches, wireframing, prototyping

Problem

Travelers find the current trip planning apps on the market time consuming, having too many steps, and leave them feeling unorganized. These travelers want to shorten the planning phase and need a place to keep all of their itinerary together so they can spend more time enjoying their trips.

Solution

Todays travelers are busy and accustomed to quicker on demand digital integrations into their daily lives. MyTripâ€™s goal is to simplify booking, planning and organizing a travelers next trip by creating one stop platform for all travel booking and planning needs.
